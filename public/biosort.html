Global Biomedical Waste Challenge

Biomedical waste poses a significant global challenge, with healthcare facilities worldwide generating millions of tons of hazardous waste annually. Improper management leads to severe environmental contamination, public health risks, and regulatory compliance issues.

5.9M Tons of biomedical waste generated globally per year
85% Of healthcare waste is non-hazardous but mixed with hazardous waste
15% Is considered hazardous and requires specialized treatment
$12B Annual global cost of biomedical waste management
